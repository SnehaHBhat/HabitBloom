🌱 What is HabitBloom?

HabitBloom is a simple and colorful habit tracking web application built to help everyday people build positive daily habits. The idea is simple — track small actions every day and watch them grow into lasting lifestyle changes over time.

🌈 How HabitBloom Works

  1. Go to the Home page — Enter your name, pick your main goal and set a daily reminder time.
  2. Visit My Habits daily — Check off each habit as you complete it throughout the day.
  3. Check My Progress — See your weekly completion table, current streak and badges earned.
  4. Keep going! 🌸 — The longer your streak, the more badges you unlock and the stronger your habits become.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is HabitBloom free to use?

Yes! HabitBloom is completely free. No account or credit card needed.

How many habits can I track?

You can track up to 10 habits at a time.

Will I lose my streak if I miss a day?

Yes, but don't worry — your best streak is always saved on your progress page.

Can I use HabitBloom on my phone?

Absolutely! HabitBloom is designed to work on all screen sizes.
