💡 Tips to Improve

How do I improve my completion rate?

Try habit stacking: attach a new habit to one you already do automatically, like drinking water right after brushing your teeth.

What if I miss a day?

Don't stress! Just get back on track the next day. Missing once is human — missing twice becomes a pattern.

How do I earn more badges?

Keep your daily streak going and try hitting the 30 day streak to unlock the Legend badge!
